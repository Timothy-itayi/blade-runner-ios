# S1-03 JAMES CHEN - DENIED

## News Report Script

**Source:** AMBER NEWS NETWORK
**Tone:** NEUTRAL
**Duration:** ~30-35 seconds

---

### Script

*[News anchor tone - neutral, diplomatic]*

A routine border denial has escalated into a diplomatic incident... after James Chen, a Level Four military contractor, was refused entry for medical consultation.

His employer has filed a formal complaint, alleging... quote: "overzealous screening procedures."

AMBER officials defended the decision, citing quote: "unresolved questions regarding previous visits."

The contractor has returned to Mars Colony pending review.

---

### Voice Direction
- Neutral, balanced reporting tone
- Present both sides fairly
- "overzealous screening procedures" - read as direct quote, no editorializing
- "unresolved questions" - slight emphasis, hint there's more to the story
- Wrap up is procedural, matter-of-fact

# TestFlight Description for AMBER Beta

**AMBER** is a narrative decision-making game where you play as a security operator at a transit checkpoint. Verify subjects, investigate discrepancies, and make approval/denial decisions that shape the story.

**BETA BUILD - This is a demo, not the final game. Features, UI, and gameplay will change in future builds.**

**What to Test:**
- Complete the demo shift (3 subjects: Eva, Mara, Chen)
- Test verification flow: identity scans, credential checks, interrogation
- Make decisions (APPROVE/DENY) and see consequences
- Audio playback and settings
- End-of-shift news reports
- Overall gameplay flow and stability

**Known Limitations:**
- Limited to 3 subjects (demo scope)
- Some features may be incomplete
- UI/UX subject to change

Thank you for testing!
